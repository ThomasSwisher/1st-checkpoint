We Are Here for You

At Saltzer Health, we understand that illnesses and injuries can happen to anyone at any time. That’s why we’re here for you from 8 am to 8 pm seven days a week. If you’ve got a cold, flu, urinary symptom, cut, burn, need a sports physical or other non-emergency issues we’ve got you covered.

With no appointments needed for your urgent care needs, you can just walk in and our warm, welcoming, skilled medical team will get you back to feeling good. For current information about COVID-19 testing at Saltzer Health urgent care clinics, please visit: saltzerhealth.com/coronavirus
